Roof Cleaning

Safe soft wash cleaning that removes ugly black streaks and extends your roof's life

Say Goodbye to Black Streaks

Those dark streaks running down your roof aren't just dirt - they're actually a type of algae called Gloeocapsa magma. This bacteria thrives in Florida's humid climate and feeds on the limestone filler in your shingles. Left untreated, it can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan and reduce your home's curb appeal.

Our professional roof cleaning service uses the soft wash method recommended by shingle manufacturers like GAF and CertainTeed. This low-pressure approach safely removes algae, moss, and lichen without damaging your shingles - unlike high-pressure washing, which can strip away the protective granules and void your warranty.

Roof Types We Clean

  • Asphalt shingles
  • Tile roofs (clay and concrete)
  • Metal roofs
  • Flat roofs
  • Cedar shake (when appropriate)

Why Roof Cleaning Matters

Extend Roof Life

Algae feeds on your shingles. Removing it can add years to your roof's lifespan, saving you thousands on premature replacement.

Improve Energy Efficiency

Dark streaks absorb more heat. A clean roof reflects sunlight better, helping keep your attic cooler and reducing AC costs.

Boost Curb Appeal

A clean roof makes your entire home look better. Great for everyday enjoyment or if you're planning to sell.

Maintain Warranty

Many shingle warranties require regular maintenance. Our soft wash method is manufacturer-approved and won't void your coverage.

Our Soft Wash Process

We never use high pressure on roofs. Our process follows ARMA (Asphalt Roofing Manufacturers Association) guidelines:

1

Ground-Level Application

We apply our cleaning solution from the ground or low ladders whenever possible, minimizing roof traffic.

2

Gentle Treatment

Our solution penetrates algae colonies, killing them at the root rather than just removing surface growth.

3

Low-Pressure Rinse

We rinse with pressure equivalent to a garden hose - strong enough to clean, gentle enough to protect.

4

Plant Protection

We pre-wet and post-rinse all landscaping to ensure our eco-friendly solutions don't affect your plants.
