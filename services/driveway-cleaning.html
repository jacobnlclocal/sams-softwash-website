Driveway Cleaning

Make your concrete look like it was just poured with professional pressure washing

Restore Your Driveway's Original Beauty

Your driveway takes a beating. Oil drips from cars, tire marks accumulate, and Florida's humidity encourages mold and algae growth. Over time, what was once bright, clean concrete becomes dark, stained, and uninviting. But underneath all that grime, your driveway is still there - it just needs professional attention.

Our high-pressure concrete cleaning service uses commercial-grade equipment to blast away years of buildup. The difference is dramatic - most customers can't believe how good their driveway looks when we're done. It's like getting a brand new driveway at a fraction of the cost.

Surfaces We Clean

  • Concrete driveways
  • Paver driveways
  • Sidewalks and walkways
  • Garage floors
  • Curbs and entryways
  • Pool decks

Stains We Remove

Oil & Grease

Car leaks, cooking grease, and automotive fluids are no match for our pre-treatment solutions and high-pressure cleaning.

Tire Marks

Those black streaks from hot tires? We remove them completely, restoring your concrete's uniform appearance.

Mold & Algae

Florida's humidity causes green and black growth on concrete. We kill it at the source for longer-lasting results.

Rust Stains

Orange rust stains from sprinklers, fertilizer, or metal objects require special treatment - and we've got it.

Our Process

1

Pre-Treatment

We apply degreaser to oil stains and cleaning solution to biological growth before pressure washing.

2

High-Pressure Cleaning

Using a surface cleaner attachment, we apply consistent pressure across your driveway for streak-free results.

3

Edge Detailing

We detail edges, expansion joints, and hard-to-reach areas with our wand for complete cleaning.

4

Final Rinse

We rinse away all debris and cleaning solutions, leaving your driveway looking brand new.
