Commercial Cleaning

Keep your business looking professional with our commercial pressure washing services

Professional Image, Professional Results

First impressions matter in business. When customers approach your storefront, walk through your parking lot, or visit your office building, the cleanliness of your exterior sends a message about how you run your business. A dirty, stained exterior suggests neglect - while a clean, well-maintained property signals professionalism and attention to detail.

Sam's Softwash provides commercial pressure washing services for businesses throughout Walton County. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption, and we're fully licensed and insured for commercial work.

  • Gas stations and convenience stores

  • Warehouses and industrial facilities
  • HOA and property management

Our Commercial Services

Building Exterior

Soft wash cleaning for siding, stucco, brick, and other exterior surfaces. Remove dirt, algae, and pollution stains.

Parking Lots

High-pressure cleaning for concrete and asphalt parking areas. Remove oil stains, gum, and accumulated grime.

Sidewalks & Entrances

Keep walkways clean and safe for customers. Regular cleaning prevents slip hazards from algae and mildew.

Drive-Thrus

Specialized cleaning for drive-thru lanes, including gum removal and grease treatment for restaurant locations.

Dumpster Areas

Sanitize and deodorize dumpster pads and enclosures. Essential for restaurants and food service locations.

Fleet Washing

Keep your company vehicles looking professional with regular fleet washing services.
