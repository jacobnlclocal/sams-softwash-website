Love Your Outdoor Spaces Again

Florida's weather is perfect for outdoor living - but it's also perfect for mold, mildew, and algae growth. Your deck, patio, and fence take a beating from the humidity, rain, and sun. Over time, what was once a beautiful outdoor retreat becomes a grimy, slippery, uninviting space.

Our outdoor surface cleaning services restore your backyard's beauty. Whether you have a wood deck that's turned gray, a paver patio covered in algae, or a fence that's green with mold, we have the right cleaning method to make it look like new.

Outdoor Surfaces We Clean

Wood Decks

Pressure washing (adjusted for wood type) or soft wash for composite decking. We can prep decks for staining too.

Patios

Concrete, pavers, flagstone, travertine - we clean all patio materials with appropriate pressure and solutions.

Fences

Wood, vinyl, and aluminum fences all benefit from professional cleaning. Removes mold and restores appearance.

Pool Decks

Keep your pool area safe and inviting with regular cleaning. Removes algae that causes slippery surfaces.

Screen Enclosures

Soft wash cleaning for pool cages and lanai screens. Removes dirt and algae without damaging screens.

Outdoor Furniture

Patio furniture, pergolas, gazebos - we can clean outdoor structures and furniture during your service.

Wood Deck Restoration

Wood decks require special care. Too much pressure can damage the wood fibers, creating a fuzzy surface that splinters easily. We use:

  • Proper pressure settings - Lower PSI for soft woods, slightly higher for hardwoods
  • Wood cleaners - Solutions that brighten gray, weathered wood
  • Proper technique - Cleaning with the grain to avoid damage
  • Stain prep - If you're planning to stain, cleaning is the essential first step

Screen Enclosure Cleaning

Pool cages and screen enclosures are common in Florida, and they get dirty fast. Pollen, dirt, and algae accumulate on screens, making your outdoor space feel dingy. Our soft wash process:

  • Uses low pressure to protect delicate screens
  • Cleans aluminum frames without scratching
  • Removes years of buildup in a single visit
  • Makes your lanai feel bright and inviting again
